Enforcing Accountability

Solution Hub is Yaoundé's primary digital infrastructure for bridging the communication gap between citizens and local administrative councils.

🛡️ Smart Routing

Our proprietary "Solution Hub Easy Fix" engine analyzes report categories and locations to instantly dispatch issues to the relevant authorities (e.g., Douala III Council, Yaoundé VII Public Works).

⏱️ Forced Escalation

Reports that remain unanswered for more than 72 hours are automatically escalated to higher-level provincial authorities, ensuring that no community problem is left ignored.
